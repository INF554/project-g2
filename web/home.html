Our visualization is base on updated Austin shelter intake data from July 2013 to Nov 2016. We use time Series chart to investigate the trends of incoming and outgoing animals. We also use geomap to explore locations where adopted animals were found. Data can be displayed on animal type or time. Sankey diagram is used to reveal relationship between animal attributes and how these attributes effect adoption result. The last but not the least, we use Calendar View to display time when those animals came to and left the shelter and linked these information with animal found location. Author Vitid and Wenjia is aimed at provide multi-dimensional data visualization for shelter officer to explore the animal intake and outgoing information.
