Our visualization is based on Texas Austin Animal Shelter's incoming and outgoing dataset. The data span from October, 2013 to November, 2016. We use Time Series Chart to investigate the trend of the numbers of incoming and outgoing animals. Geographical Map is used to explore the locations where adopted animals were found. Data can be explored based on animal type or time. Sankey Diagram is used to reveal a relationship between various animal's attributes and how these attributes affect the adoption result. We use Small Multiple to explore the affectionateness between pet owners and their pet, and the difficulty of animals getting a new adopter. Last but not the least, we use Calendar View and Map to investigate any possible animal mistreatment. We aim at providing multi-dimensional data visualization which can be beneficial to shelter officers, researchers, as well as interested readers.
